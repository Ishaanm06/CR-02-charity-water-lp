We are on a mission to bring clean and safe drinking water to every person on the planet. 100% of public donations fund clean water projects. Join us in transforming lives, one well at a time.

Our team works with local partners to find the best solutions for each community. We believe everyone deserves access to clean water, and we are committed to making that a reality. Every project is tracked and reported, so you can see the impact of your support.

Since we started, we have helped thousands of communities get access to safe water. We invite you to learn more about our story and join our growing family of supporters.

üö∞ Our Projects

From hand-dug wells to rainwater catchments, we partner with local experts to build sustainable solutions tailored to each community. With every completed project, a new chapter of hope begins.

We use different methods to bring water to people, depending on what works best in each area. Some projects use wells, while others use pumps or special filters. We also teach communities how to take care of their new water sources, so they last for many years.

Our projects are designed to be safe for children and easy for families to use. We share updates and photos from the field, so you can see the difference your support makes.
